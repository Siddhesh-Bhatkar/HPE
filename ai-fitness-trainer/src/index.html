AI Fitness Trainer

Perform exercises in front of your camera and get real-time feedback

Select an exercise to begin

How to use:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Select an exercise from the controls
  3. Position yourself so your full body is visible
  4. Perform the exercise with proper form
  5. The app will count your reps automatically

Supported Exercises:

  • Squats - Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Push-ups - Position camera to see your side profile
  • Plank - Hold a straight body position
  • Bicep Curls - Lift weights or objects up and down
